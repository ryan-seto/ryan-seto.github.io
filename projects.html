Pygame

I created my own version of the classic 1978 arcade game: Space Invaders! This game was coded in Pygame as my first ever software project. The basis of the games is to use the left and right arrow keys to move your ship and shoot space invaders. By, pressing the space bar, you can shoot down enemies. Don't let them get to the bottom of the screen or you lose!
